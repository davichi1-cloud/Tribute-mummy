Life Story

Early Life

Pastor Mrs. Omosehin Olayinka Omolara, née Olayinka Omolara Oluwadamilare Abike Kudirat, was born on 14th July 1967 to Pa Fasasi Adenuga Osinaike and Madam Mosunmola Osinaike, née Olusegun.

Her father was from Itun Agbon Efiyen, Odogbolu, while her mother was from Itantebo in Ijebu-Ode.

Due to her father's work as a civil servant and produce inspector in the cocoa industry, the family frequently relocated, exposing her to different communities and schools during her formative years.

Education

Her educational journey began at Mushin Public School, Lagos, in 1972 and continued through several schools including HLA Primary School, Anglican Primary School Ogudu, Wesley Primary School, and Saint Cyprian Catholic School in Ibadan.

She later attended Oku Owa Multilateral Secondary School and subsequently Itamerin Comprehensive High School, Oru Ago-Iwoye, where she completed her secondary education in 1984.

After secondary school, she joined her senior brother in Oyo Town and worked at Saint Lucia Hospital as a receptionist and accounts clerk, demonstrating diligence and responsibility from an early age.

Family

She met Idowu Bamidele Omosehin in 1989 and they were united in marriage in 1992.

Their union was blessed with children including Anne Oluwadamilare, David Temiloluwa Olasubomi Oluwasemilogo, and Victor Oluwademilade Adedamola.

In 2006, God further enlarged the family with the addition of Tayo Adenuga and Tumininu Adenuga.

Her family remained one of her greatest blessings and she devoted herself wholeheartedly to nurturing and supporting them.

Service & Ministry

She joined the Redeemed Christian Church of God in 1996, was baptized the same year, and immediately became an active worker in God's vineyard.

Through dedication and commitment, she progressed from Deaconess to Parish Pastor, Assistant Pastor, Area Pastor, and eventually a fully ordained Pastor.

In 2025, she became a Zonal Pastor in the Redeemed Christian Church of God, faithfully serving until her final days.

Pastor Mrs. Omosehin lived a life marked by faith, compassion, leadership, and unwavering devotion to God and humanity.

She peacefully transitioned to glory on 15th June 2026, leaving behind a legacy of love, service, and godly impact that will continue to inspire generations.

In Loving Memory of Pastor Mrs. Omosehin Olayinka Omolara
